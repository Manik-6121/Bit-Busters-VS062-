What makes us different

Trust

We ensure that each Vehicle is in good condition. Every vehicle is inspected before and after a Trip. And the Possible reasons for breakdown is removed before the Trip. Thorough Service of the Vehicles is done after each Trip.

Vehicle Tracking

Each vehicle is Tracked after it is handed over to the Customer. If found anything suspicious, the Vehicle is remotely disabled. A tracking Id is also provided to the customer for a relax Mind, conserning the safety of the vehicle.

Free First Fuel

A Full tank of Gas is provided to the Customer when handing Over the Vehicle, as a token of Thanking the Customer for choosing Us. This Price of Fuel is not Inclusive in the Final Price of the Rental.

If by anychance a customer's Vehicle Breaks Down due to some Reasons then, The Customer will be provided to continue with their Experience.
